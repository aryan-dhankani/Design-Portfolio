Description

Scrappy Squirrels is an innovative NFT project aiming to revolutionize web3 patronship and education.

It's not merely a collection of digital avatars, but a pioneering platform combining community support with tangible rewards. The project seeks to educate and facilitate the transition of artists, developers, and entrepreneurs into the web3 realm through a wealth of resources and tools. At its heart, Scrappy Squirrels is experimenting with a model where NFTs serve as a means for fans to support creators, gaining exclusive community access and potential future rewards. Additionally, it explores the potential for decentralized narrative media, where creative works can be shared and expanded within a community-driven framework. Scrappy Squirrels invites participation either through NFT purchase or by joining its community, focusing on collaborative growth and the democratization of digital patronship.
